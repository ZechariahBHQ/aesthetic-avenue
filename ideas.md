## Idea A — "Warm Travertine Editorial"

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Luxury Editorial (think Vogue Living meets Aesop)

**Core Principles:**
1. Warm off-white and travertine stone tones as the dominant canvas — never cold white
2. Generous negative space used as a luxury signal, not emptiness
3. Every typographic moment is intentional — large display serifs anchor sections, small caps for labels
4. Photography is the hero; layout frames it rather than competes with it

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: `#F5F0EA` (warm parchment) — conveys warmth and calm without clinical coldness
- Foreground text: `#2C2520` (deep espresso) — grounded, readable, premium
- Accent: `#B89A7A` (warm bronze/sand) — used sparingly for CTAs, dividers, hover states
- Secondary surface: `#EDE8E0` (lighter travertine) for card backgrounds
- Emotional intent: the palette should feel like stepping into a warm, softly lit treatment room

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric editorial grid — hero image bleeds left, text anchored right with generous margin
- Services presented in a horizontal scroll strip on desktop, stacked on mobile
- About section uses a split: large portrait left, copy right with a thin bronze rule separating them
- No centered hero text — headlines always left-aligned or offset

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin horizontal rules in bronze as section dividers and decorative accents
2. Oversized italic serif display text for section openers ("Your skin, elevated.")
3. Subtle grain/noise texture overlay on hero sections for depth

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover on service cards reveals a soft fade-in of the "Book Now" button
- Navigation links underline with a slow-draw animation on hover
- Scroll-triggered fade-up entrances for text blocks (staggered 60ms)

**Animation:**
- Entrance: `opacity 0→1` + `translateY(20px→0)` at 400ms ease-out
- CTA button: scale(0.97) on active, 160ms ease-out
- No parallax — too distracting for a calm, luxury feel

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Cormorant Garamond` — Italic weight for hero headlines, Regular for section titles
- Body: `DM Sans` — Light 300 for body copy, Medium 500 for labels and CTAs
- Hierarchy: 72px display → 36px section title → 18px body → 12px label/caption

## Idea B — "Minimal Japandi Clinic"

**Design Movement:** Japandi Minimalism (Japanese restraint + Scandinavian warmth)

**Core Principles:**
1. Maximum restraint — if an element doesn't serve the user, remove it
2. Monochromatic palette with a single warm accent
3. Typography does all the heavy lifting; imagery is used once, powerfully
4. Whitespace is the luxury

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: `#FAFAF8` (near-white with warmth)
- Text: `#1A1A18` (near-black)
- Accent: `#C4A882` (muted gold)
- Surfaces: `#F0EDE8`
- Intent: clinical precision softened by warmth

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Single-column narrative scroll with deliberate wide margins (20% each side on desktop)
- Services as a numbered list with large numerals as decorative elements
- No cards, no grids — pure typographic hierarchy

**Signature Elements:**
1. Large numerals (01, 02, 03) in light weight as section markers
2. Single full-bleed image per page, used once
3. Hairline borders only

**Interaction Philosophy:** Instant, no animation. Hover = colour shift only.

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Playfair Display` Italic
- Body: `Jost` Light
- Hierarchy: 64px → 32px → 16px → 11px

## Idea C — "Soft Feminine Botanica"

**Design Movement:** Modern Feminine Botanica (Aerin Lauder meets spa editorial)

**Core Principles:**
1. Soft blush and sage as the primary palette — feminine without being cliché
2. Organic shapes — rounded containers, soft curves, no hard corners
3. Layered depth using soft shadows and translucent overlays
4. Warmth through texture: linen-like backgrounds, subtle noise

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: `#FBF7F4` (warm cream)
- Primary: `#8B6F5E` (dusty rose-brown)
- Accent: `#C9A99A` (blush)
- Surface: `#F2EBE6`
- Intent: inviting, warm, feminine, luxurious

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Overlapping card layout — service cards slightly overlap the hero image
- Rounded containers (24px radius) throughout
- Soft drop shadows on all cards

**Signature Elements:**
1. Soft blush gradient overlays on hero images
2. Rounded pill-shaped CTA buttons
3. Subtle botanical SVG line art as section decoration

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Libre Baskerville` Italic
- Body: `Nunito` Light
- Hierarchy: 68px → 34px → 17px → 12px

## Selected Direction: **Idea A — Warm Travertine Editorial**

This direction best matches the brand positioning: luxury, calm, results-driven, and elevated. The warm travertine palette avoids the clinical coldness of white while remaining sophisticated. The asymmetric editorial layout differentiates Aesthetic Avenue from generic beauty clinic sites. Cormorant Garamond brings the high-fashion serif energy seen in the inspiration references (Dolce Vita, Blanc Skin), while DM Sans keeps body copy clean and readable on mobile.
